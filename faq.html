Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Get in touch and we'll answer your question directly.

What services does Guided Tech Solutions offer?

We offer IT support, business process automation, AI consulting, machine learning solutions, cloud migration, RPA (Robotic Process Automation) setup, integration tools, custom connector development, and technology consulting for businesses and government organizations.

How can business automation help my company?

Business automation removes repetitive manual tasks from your daily operations — things like data entry, follow-ups, file transfers, and report generation. This saves time, reduces errors, and frees your team to focus on revenue-generating work. We design custom automation workflows tailored to your specific business processes.

What is RPA and how is it different from regular automation?

RPA (Robotic Process Automation) uses software bots to automate tasks in systems that don't have APIs or modern integration points — like legacy software, older databases, or web portals. The bots interact with these systems just like a human would: logging in, copying data, filling forms, and submitting entries. This makes automation possible without replacing your existing tools.

What is AI consulting and does my business need it?

AI consulting helps you identify where artificial intelligence can genuinely improve your business — and where it can't. We assess your operations, data, and goals to design practical AI solutions that drive real results like faster decisions, better predictions, and reduced manual work. If you're curious about AI but not sure where to start, a consultation is the right first step.

How does cloud migration work and how long does it take?

Cloud migration involves moving your data, applications, and infrastructure from on-premise or legacy systems to a modern cloud platform like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud. We start with a full assessment of your current environment, then plan and execute the migration in phases to minimize disruption. Timelines vary based on complexity — small projects may take a few weeks, while larger enterprise migrations can span several months.

Does Guided Tech Solutions work with government agencies?

Yes. We are an SDVOSB (Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business) certified company with experience delivering IT solutions for government and mission-driven organizations. We are aligned with CJIS, NIST 800-53, and CMMC compliance standards.

How do I get started with Guided Tech Solutions?

The easiest way to get started is to book a free 30-minute strategy call. We'll listen to your situation, assess your needs, and map out the best path forward — with no commitment required.

What size businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes — from small businesses and startups to mid-sized companies and enterprise organizations, as well as municipalities and school districts. Our solutions are tailored to your specific needs and budget.
