WHAT WE ARE

We help global brands deliver great results

We simplify complex technology challenges, improve operational efficiency, and help organizations grow through innovative, dependable solutions. Our reliable systems empower clients to confidently navigate an evolving technology landscape.

Our mission is to modernize Mississippi's critical infrastructure one city, one school, and one business at a time. We deliver secure, compliant, and resilient technology with zero downtime and zero overwhelm, ensuring readiness for CJIS, NIST, and CMMC standards.

Our vision is to empower organizations through secure, scalable digital transformation that drives innovation, resilience, and long-term sustainability.

Starting in Mississippi and extending beyond, we envision a future where cities, schools, and businesses operate on modern, compliant, and dependable technology without disruption, complexity, or compromise.

Guided Tech Solutions was built out of necessity, not trends. Across Mississippi, we kept encountering the same problem.

Cities, schools, and businesses were running critical operations on aging servers, manual processes, and fragile networks. Systems were slow.

We saw the gap, and we filled it. As cybersecurity threats grew, so did the risk. Major technology providers weren't stepping in. So we did.

Guided Tech Solutions was founded to help organizations modernize without disruption — moving them to the cloud, strengthening infrastructure, automating what slows them down, and providing reliable, human-centered technical support.
